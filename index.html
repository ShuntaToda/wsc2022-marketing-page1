Top 4 Features showcasing

Game Version

Select a specific version or release of the game. Players may choose different versions of the game, allowing for the addition of new features or fixes to past bugs.

Highscore Leaderboard

Display the highest scores achieved by players within the game. It foster competition and allows players to compare their performances with others

User Profile to Showcase High Scores

Share your high scores and game achievements with other players or friends. Profiles typically include a player's nickname, avatar, and attained accomplishments.

Game Ranking

Show where players stand in the game based on performance metrics. This ranking system often highlights top performers and motivates players to improve their standings.
